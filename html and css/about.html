The Story of Canva

Canva was founded in 2012 with the goal of empowering everyone to create beautiful designs. The website has grown over the years, and it now offers a wide range of features and tools to help users create stunning visuals.

Our Mission and Values

At Canva, we are committed to providing our users with the best possible design experience. Our mission is to be a trusted and accessible platform that enables everyone to express their creativity and ideas.

  • Simplicity and ease of use
  • Creativity and innovation
  • Inclusivity and accessibility
  • Sustainability and environmental responsibility
